# Hello World

Welcome to XsysCloud — my corner of the internet where I write about IT infrastructure, cloud, automation, and everything I'm learning along the way.

## What to expect here

- **Scripting & Automation** — PowerShell and Bash scripts that solve real problems

- **Development** — Building things with Next.js, React, and whatever else I pick up

## Why blog?

If you're an IT professional, a developer, or someone trying to break into tech from the sysadmin side — welcome. Let's learn together.
